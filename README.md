# ai marketing workshop
Prompt for AI Marketing Workshop
A hands-on 2-hour session for the internal marketing and communications team, aimed at those with intermediate AI experience. 

It walks through a full creative workflow — ideation, product design, branding, imagery, and video — across six tools: Google Stitch/Claude Design, Gemini (with Copilot as a backup), Claude, Adobe Firefly/Nano Banana, and Google Flow. 

Each block pairs a short demo with a guided exercise applied to real team use cases, including building a design system with Claude and ready-made prompts for product imagery. The goal: every participant leaves with a repeatable workflow, not just theory.
